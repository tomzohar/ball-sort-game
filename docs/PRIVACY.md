# Privacy Policy — Ball Sort

_Last updated: 2026-06-27_

Ball Sort is a single-player puzzle game. We designed it to collect **nothing**.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Ball Sort does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

- No account, sign-in, or registration.
- No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
- No third-party SDKs of any kind.
- No network requests — the app works fully offline.

## Data stored on your device

Your gameplay data — current level, progress, statistics, daily streak, and
settings (sound and haptics toggles) — is saved **only on your device** using
Apple's standard on-device storage. It never leaves your device, and we cannot
see it. Deleting the app removes this data.

## Children

Ball Sort collects no data from anyone, including children, and contains no ads,
no purchases, and no external links.

## Changes

If a future version of the app changes what data is handled, this policy and the
App Store privacy label will be updated before that version ships.
